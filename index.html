Love Stars!

I'm a fan of stars, often find myself looking at them. Well, when I'm out of valley at least cause it's not that visible inside the valley.These are some of the pictures I took while I was out, took it with my smartphone actually. Not a photographer but I think I managed to take some good shots of it.

Stars that are light years away from us could actually be in a completely different state than how we see them now because we only see the light that left them years and years ago.

UY Scuti, a star that's about 9500 lightyears away from us takes about 9500 years for the light from there to reach us here, which means we see the UY Scuti how it was about 9500 years ago.

Imagine UY Scuti sitting right where the sun is. It is 1700 times larger than our sun. It would reach jupiter just sitting where our sun is. Love space.
